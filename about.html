1972

BMW M IS BORN.

Created as a division of BMW’s racing teams, BMW Motorsport roars onto the scene. Only one year later, the BMW 3.0 CSL wins the Nürburgring 24 Hours.

1978

BMW M1

As the namesake of BMW M vehicles, the BMW M1 launches the career of the letter “M” that is destined to be a synonym for top performance and success.

1983

BMW WINS THE FORMULA ONE WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP.

BMW wins its first title in a Brabham BMW driven by Nelson Piquet. The winning vehicle has a BMW turbo engine – producing an incredible 790 hp.

1984

BMW M635 CSI.

As the first luxury sportscar of its kind, the BMW M635 CSI perfectly embodies elegance and power on wheels.

BMW M5.

Making history, this sportscar becomes the first street-legal high-performance sedan.

1986

BMW M3.

The first BMW M3 is introduced as a street version of the champion touring car – bringing motorsport performance to everyday roads.

1988

BMW M coupe.

the 321 M coupé brings the great tradition of BMW two-seaters sportscars back to life.

BMW M5.

The third generation of the M5 debuts as one of the most powerful vehicles ever built by BMW. It's also the first to be powered by an impressive 8-cylinder engine capable of producing 400 hp.
